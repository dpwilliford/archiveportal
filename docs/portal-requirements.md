# Archive Portal Requirements

The Portal renders approved public exports from Archive OS.

## Required Modules

- Public finding aids
- Hierarchical collection trees
- Container lists
- Digital object viewers
- IIIF manifests and viewers
- Oral history presentation
- Search and faceted browse
- Authority and subject pages
- Exhibits, essays, and timelines
- Rights-controlled downloads
- Public impact dashboard

## Prohibited Content

The Portal must never expose:

- Preservation masters
- Donor legal identities
- Restricted metadata
- Confidential agreements
- Internal processing notes
- Private audit logs
