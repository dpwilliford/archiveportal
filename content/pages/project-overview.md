The Sarasota LGBT Archive is a long-term community project to preserve and share the history of LGBTQ life in Sarasota.

The project combines oral history, archival preservation, historical research, writing, exhibitions, and digital publishing.

Our long-term goal is to build a preservation-grade archival infrastructure and a public platform that will continue to grow over decades as new stories, records, and research are added.

The first public launch focuses on establishing a visible institutional presence and inviting participation from donors, volunteers, and oral history participants.
