The Sarasota LGBT Archive is a community-centered archive dedicated to preserving, documenting, and sharing the histories of LGBTQ people in Sarasota and the surrounding region.

The archive is being built to support long-term preservation, public history, oral history, publications, exhibitions, and community memory.

This public website is the archive's first public-facing portal. It is not the preservation repository itself. Preservation masters, donor records, restricted records, and technical preservation documentation remain outside this website in the separate Archive OS planning environment.

## What We Are Building

We are building a permanent archive for photographs, publications, oral histories, organizational records, personal papers, ephemera, digital materials, and community memory.

## What This Website Does Now

At launch, this website introduces the project, invites support, receives basic inquiries, and provides a public contact point for people interested in volunteering, donating, funding, or sharing oral histories.

## What This Website Does Not Yet Do

This website does not yet accept direct uploads of archival materials. Please do not send sensitive records, private files, confidential donor information, or archival deposits through the contact form.
