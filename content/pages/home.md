## Our Mission

The Sarasota LGBT Archive is building a permanent community archive to document and preserve the history of LGBTQ people in Sarasota and the surrounding region.

## Support the Archive

We are seeking donors and funding supporters to help establish the archive's technical infrastructure, oral history program, and public website.

## Volunteer

We welcome volunteers interested in archival processing, oral history, research, writing, and community outreach.

## Share an Oral History

We invite community members to participate in recorded oral histories.

## Important Note

The website is not yet accepting direct uploads of archival materials. Please use the contact form to discuss donations or sensitive materials.
